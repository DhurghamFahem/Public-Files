# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

Welcome to **Mafia Night**.  
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
We may collect limited personal information when you choose to register or log in using social authentication providers (such as Google), including:
- Display name
- Profile photo (if provided by the provider)
- User ID provided by the authentication service

We do **not** collect or store passwords directly.

---

### 1.2 Non-Personal Information
We may automatically collect non-personal information such as:
- Device type and operating system
- App usage data (screens visited, game phases)
- Crash reports and performance logs

This information is used only to improve the stability and experience of the app.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Create and manage user accounts
- Enable online multiplayer gameplay
- Create, join, and manage game rooms
- Maintain fair gameplay
- Improve performance, stability, and user experience
- Display advertisements where applicable

---

## 3. Advertisements

Mafia Night uses **Google AdMob** to display ads.

AdMob may collect:
- Advertising ID
- Device information
- Usage data for analytics and ad delivery

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your data may be shared only with trusted third-party services used to operate the app, including:
- Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics)
- Google AdMob (advertising and analytics)

These services are used strictly to provide and improve the app experience.

---

## 5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:
- Secure authentication methods
- Encrypted network communication
- Restricted access to backend services

However, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Mafia Night is **not intended for children under the age of 13**.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.  
If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

---

## 7. Your Choices

You can:
- Log out of your account
- Delete your account (if supported by the app)
- Disable personalized ads from your device settings
- Manage app permissions through your device settings
